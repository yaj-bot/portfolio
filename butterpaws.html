Butterpaws

A guided companion app for new pet parents. It mirrors the journey — high-touch in the first few months when anxiety is highest, then fading into a steady support tool as you find your footing.

Going live 30 July 2026. Built solo on Expo, React Native, and Supabase.
  • Pet profile that makes every reminder and recommendation specific to your animal
  • Vaccination, deworming, and vet visit tracking
  • Onboarding checklist for the first 30 days
  • Weekly guidance that adapts as your pet grows
  • Document storage for health records
